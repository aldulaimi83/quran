Surah Al-Kahf — The Cave

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ كَانَتْ لَهُمْ جَنَّاتُ الْفِرْدَوْسِ نُزُلًا

Simple Meaning

Recommended to recite every Friday, this surah tells four stories: the People of the Cave, the man with two gardens, Musa and Khidr, and Dhul-Qarnayn. Each story addresses a trial — of faith, wealth, knowledge, and power.

Key Lessons

  • Recite Al-Kahf every Friday as protection from the Dajjal (hadith).
  • Say: If the sea were ink for writing the words of my Lord, the sea would run dry (18:109).
  • Wealth and children are the adornment of worldly life — but good deeds that endure are better (18:46).
