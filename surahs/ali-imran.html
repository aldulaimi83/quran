Surah Ali 'Imran — Family of Imran

اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ

Simple Meaning

This surah covers the stories of Maryam, Isa (Jesus), Zakariya, and the family of Imran. It addresses the battles of Badr and Uhud and calls believers to unity, patience, and trust in Allah.

Key Lessons

  • Hold firmly to the rope of Allah together and do not be divided (3:103).
  • True believers are those who remember Allah standing, sitting, and on their sides (3:191).
  • After every hardship, put your trust in Allah alone (3:159).
