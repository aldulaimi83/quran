Surah 46 — الأحقاف

Surah Al-Ahqaf — The Sandhills

Read Surah Al-Ahqaf online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 46 of the Holy Quran — The Sandhills — 35 verses.

حم ۝ تَنزِيلُ الْكِتَابِ مِنَ اللَّهِ الْعَزِيزِ الْحَكِيمِ

Simple Meaning

This surah speaks of the people of 'Ad who lived among the sandhills and were destroyed for their arrogance. It calls people to honour their parents and do righteous deeds, and mentions the jinn who listened to the Quran and believed.

Key Lessons

  • We commanded man to be good to his parents (46:15).
  • And when We directed a group of jinn towards you to listen to the Quran — when they attended it they said: Listen quietly (46:29).
  • My Lord, enable me to be grateful for Your blessing which You have bestowed upon me and upon my parents (46:15).
