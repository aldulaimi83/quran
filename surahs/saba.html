Surah 34 — سبأ

Surah Saba — Sheba

Read Surah Saba online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 34 of the Holy Quran — Sheba — 54 verses.

وَلَقَدْ آتَيْنَا دَاوُودَ مِنَّا فَضْلًا

Simple Meaning

This surah tells of the blessings given to Dawud and Sulayman, and the story of the people of Saba — who had fertile gardens and were commanded to be grateful but turned away. It warns that ingratitude leads to ruin.

Key Lessons

  • Work, O family of Dawud, in gratitude — and few of My servants are truly grateful (34:13).
  • Say: Verily my Lord expands provision for whoever He wills and restricts it — but most people do not know (34:36).
  • Nothing of the weight of an atom escapes Him in the heavens or on earth (34:3).
