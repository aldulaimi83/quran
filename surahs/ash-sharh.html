Surah 94 — الشرح

Surah Ash-Sharh — The Relief

Read Surah Ash-Sharh online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 94 of the Holy Quran — The Relief — 8 verses.

أَلَمْ نَشْرَحْ لَكَ صَدْرَكَ

Simple Meaning

This very short surah is a profound comfort. Allah asks the Prophet: Did We not expand your chest, remove your burden, and raise your mention? And it promises: after every difficulty comes ease — twice.

Key Lessons

  • Did We not expand your chest for you? (94:1).
  • Truly with hardship comes ease — truly with hardship comes ease (94:5-6).
  • So when you have finished, stand up for worship — and turn to your Lord with longing (94:7-8).
