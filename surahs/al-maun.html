Surah 107

Surah Al-Ma'un - Translation and Tafsir

Surah Al-Ma'un warns against empty religion without mercy, sincerity, and care for people in need.

أَرَأَيْتَ الَّذِي يُكَذِّبُ بِالدِّينِ
فَذَٰلِكَ الَّذِي يَدُعُّ الْيَتِيمَ
وَلَا يَحُضُّ عَلَىٰ طَعَامِ الْمِسْكِينِ
فَوَيْلٌ لِّلْمُصَلِّينَ
الَّذِينَ هُمْ عَن صَلَاتِهِمْ سَاهُونَ
الَّذِينَ هُمْ يُرَاءُونَ
وَيَمْنَعُونَ الْمَاعُونَ

Simple Meaning

This surah criticizes people who neglect orphans, ignore the poor, pray carelessly, show off, and refuse small acts of help.

Key Lessons

  • Faith should make us kinder to others.
  • Prayer should be sincere, not for showing off.
  • Small acts of help matter to Allah.
