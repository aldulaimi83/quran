نَحْنُ نَقُصُّ عَلَيْكَ أَحْسَنَ الْقَصَصِ

Simple Meaning

Called the best of all stories in the Quran, this surah tells the full life of Yusuf (Joseph) — from being thrown in the well by his brothers to becoming the treasurer of Egypt. It teaches patience, chastity, and trust in Allah's plan.

Key Lessons

  • We relate to you the best of stories (12:3).
  • Indeed, no one despairs of the mercy of Allah except those who disbelieve (12:87).
  • His Lord responded to him and turned away their plot — He is the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing (12:34).
