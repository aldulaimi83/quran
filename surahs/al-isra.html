Surah 17 — الإسراء

Surah Al-Isra — The Night Journey

Read Surah Al-Isra online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 17 of the Holy Quran — The Night Journey — 111 verses.

سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي أَسْرَىٰ بِعَبْدِهِ لَيْلًا

Simple Meaning

Beginning with the miraculous Night Journey of the Prophet from Makkah to Jerusalem, this surah covers the Ten Commandments of Islam, the importance of prayer, and the Quran as a healing and mercy for believers.

Key Lessons

  • Glory be to Him who took His servant on a journey by night (17:1).
  • Your Lord has decreed that you worship none but Him, and show kindness to your parents (17:23).
  • And say: My Lord, increase me in knowledge (17:80).
