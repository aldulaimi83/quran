Surah 102 — التكاثر

Surah At-Takathur — The Rivalry for Increase

Read Surah At-Takathur online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 102 of the Holy Quran — The Rivalry for Increase — 8 verses.

أَلْهَاكُمُ التَّكَاثُرُ ۝ حَتَّىٰ زُرْتُمُ الْمَقَابِرَ

Simple Meaning

This surah warns against the distraction of competing to accumulate more — wealth, children, status — until you end up in the grave. It warns that you will be questioned about every blessing you were given, and that you will see the Hellfire.

Key Lessons

  • Competition in worldly increase distracts you — until you visit the graves (102:1-2).
  • If only you knew with certain knowledge — you would see the Hellfire (102:5-6).
  • Then on that Day you will be asked about every blessing (102:8).
