Surah 45 — الجاثية

Surah Al-Jathiyah — The Crouching

Read Surah Al-Jathiyah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 45 of the Holy Quran — The Crouching — 37 verses.

حم ۝ تَنزِيلُ الْكِتَابِ مِنَ اللَّهِ الْعَزِيزِ الْحَكِيمِ

Simple Meaning

This surah describes the signs of Allah in creation and warns those who reject them. It tells of the Day when every nation will be summoned kneeling before Allah, and every soul will be repaid for what it did.

Key Lessons

  • And you will see every nation kneeling down — every nation will be called to its record (45:28).
  • Whoever does a righteous deed — it is for their own soul, and whoever does evil — it is against it (45:15).
  • Leave them to their amusement and idle talk until they meet their Day which they are promised (43:83).
