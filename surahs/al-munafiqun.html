Surah 63 — المنافقون

Surah Al-Munafiqun — The Hypocrites

Read Surah Al-Munafiqun online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 63 of the Holy Quran — The Hypocrites — 11 verses.

إِذَا جَاءَكَ الْمُنَافِقُونَ قَالُوا نَشْهَدُ إِنَّكَ لَرَسُولُ اللَّهِ

Simple Meaning

This surah exposes the hypocrites of Madinah, who outwardly professed Islam but inwardly rejected it. It warns against being deceived by their speech and reminds believers not to let wealth and children distract them from remembering Allah.

Key Lessons

  • When the hypocrites come to you they say: We testify that you are the Messenger of Allah — and Allah knows that you are His messenger and Allah testifies that the hypocrites are liars (63:1).
  • O believers, do not let your wealth or your children distract you from the remembrance of Allah (63:9).
  • Spend from what We have provided you before death comes to one of you (63:10).
