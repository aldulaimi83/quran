Surah 98 — البينة

Surah Al-Bayyinah — The Clear Evidence

Read Surah Al-Bayyinah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 98 of the Holy Quran — The Clear Evidence — 8 verses.

لَمْ يَكُنِ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْ أَهْلِ الْكِتَابِ وَالْمُشْرِكِينَ مُنفَكِّينَ

Simple Meaning

This surah addresses the People of the Book and the polytheists who did not stop their ways until clear evidence came to them — a messenger from Allah reciting purified pages. It declares that the best of creatures are those who believe and do righteous deeds.

Key Lessons

  • The disbelievers among the People of the Book would not have stopped until the clear evidence came to them (98:1).
  • They were not commanded except to worship Allah sincerely — establish prayer and give zakat (98:5).
  • The best of creatures are those who believe and do righteous deeds — their reward with their Lord is eternal gardens (98:7-8).
