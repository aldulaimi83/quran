Surah Ya-Sin — Ya-Sin

يس ۝ وَالْقُرْآنِ الْحَكِيمِ ۝ إِنَّكَ لَمِنَ الْمُرْسَلِينَ

Simple Meaning

Known as the heart of the Quran, Ya-Sin speaks of the Quran, prophethood, the story of the companions of the city, the signs of Allah in nature, and a powerful description of the Day of Resurrection.

Key Lessons

  • Ya-Sin — by the wise Quran, truly you are among the messengers (36:1-3).
  • Is not He who created the heavens and the earth capable of creating the like of them? (36:81).
  • His command when He wills a thing is only to say to it: Be — and it is (36:82).
