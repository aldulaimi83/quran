Surah 84 — الانشقاق

Surah Al-Inshiqaq — The Sundering

Read Surah Al-Inshiqaq online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 84 of the Holy Quran — The Sundering — 25 verses.

إِذَا السَّمَاءُ انشَقَّتْ

Simple Meaning

This surah describes the sky splitting, the earth laid flat, and then the receiving of records on Judgment Day. One group receives their record in the right hand and rejoices; the other calls for destruction. It ends with an oath that man will progress stage by stage.

Key Lessons

  • When the sky is rent asunder and obeys its Lord as it must (84:1-2).
  • As for one given his record in his right hand — he will have an easy reckoning (84:7-8).
  • You will surely travel from stage to stage (84:19).
