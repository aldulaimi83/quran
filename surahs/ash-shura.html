Surah 42 — الشورى

Surah Ash-Shura — The Consultation

Read Surah Ash-Shura online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 42 of the Holy Quran — The Consultation — 53 verses.

وَمَا أَصَابَكُم مِّن مُّصِيبَةٍ فَبِمَا كَسَبَتْ أَيْدِيكُمْ

Simple Meaning

This surah emphasises that consultation (shura) is a quality of believers. It explains that whatever misfortune strikes you is from your own hands, and ends with a powerful passage about how Allah speaks to people — through revelation, from behind a veil, or via a messenger.

Key Lessons

  • Whatever misfortune strikes you, it is because of what your own hands have earned — and He pardons much (42:30).
  • Among His signs is the creation of the heavens and earth, and whatever living creatures He has spread in them (42:29).
  • There is nothing comparable to Him — and He is the All-Hearing, the All-Seeing (42:11).
