Surah 87 — الأعلى

Surah Al-A'la — The Most High

Read Surah Al-A'la online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 87 of the Holy Quran — The Most High — 19 verses.

سَبِّحِ اسْمَ رَبِّكَ الْأَعْلَى ۝ الَّذِي خَلَقَ فَسَوَّىٰ

Simple Meaning

The Prophet used to recite this surah in Witr and Friday prayers. It praises the name of Allah the Most High who created and proportioned, decreed and guided, brought forth green pasture and turned it to dry stubble.

Key Lessons

  • Glorify the name of your Lord, the Most High — who created and proportioned (87:1-2).
  • We will make you recite, and you will not forget — except what Allah wills (87:6-7).
  • Successful is the one who purifies himself and remembers the name of his Lord and prays (87:14-15).
