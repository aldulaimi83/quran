Surah 2 — البقرة

Surah Al-Baqarah — The Cow

Read Surah Al-Baqarah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 2 of the Holy Quran — The Cow — 286 verses.

ذَٰلِكَ الْكِتَابُ لَا رَيْبَ فِيهِ هُدًى لِلْمُتَّقِينَ

Simple Meaning

The longest surah in the Quran covers belief, prayer, fasting, charity, the story of Adam and Iblis, Bani Israel, and many laws of Islam. Its final two verses (Al-Baqarah 285–286) are among the most powerful in the Quran.

Key Lessons

  • This Book has no doubt in it — it is guidance for those who are mindful of Allah.
  • The Throne Verse (Ayat al-Kursi, 2:255) is the greatest verse in the Quran.
  • Allah does not burden a soul beyond what it can bear (2:286).
