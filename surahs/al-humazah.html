Surah 104 — الهمزة

Surah Al-Humazah — The Slanderer

Read Surah Al-Humazah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 104 of the Holy Quran — The Slanderer — 9 verses.

وَيْلٌ لِّكُلِّ هُمَزَةٍ لُّمَزَةٍ

Simple Meaning

This surah warns against those who mock and belittle others, who hoard wealth thinking it will make them live forever. Their destination is the Crusher — a fire that rises over the hearts of those who locked themselves in.

Key Lessons

  • Woe to every slanderer and backbiter (104:1).
  • Who collects wealth and counts it — thinking that his wealth will make him immortal (104:2-3).
  • No — he will surely be thrown into the Crusher — and what will make you know what the Crusher is? (104:4-5).
