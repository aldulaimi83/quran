Surah Al-Qasas — The Stories

إِنَّ الَّذِي فَرَضَ عَلَيْكَ الْقُرْآنَ لَرَادُّكَ إِلَىٰ مَعَادٍ

Simple Meaning

This surah tells the story of Musa in great detail — his birth, rescue by Pharaoh's family, his flight to Madyan, his marriage, and his return to Egypt with prophethood. It connects this story to the Prophet's own situation in Makkah.

Key Lessons

  • He who made the Quran obligatory for you will surely return you to a place of return (28:85).
  • Do not seek to cause corruption on earth — Allah does not love the corrupters (28:77).
  • Whatever good you spend is for your own souls — spend only seeking the Face of Allah (2:272).
