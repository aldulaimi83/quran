Surah 111 — المسد

Surah Al-Masad — The Palm Fibre

Read Surah Al-Masad online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 111 of the Holy Quran — The Palm Fibre — 5 verses.

تَبَّتْ يَدَا أَبِي لَهَبٍ وَتَبَّ

Simple Meaning

This surah condemns Abu Lahab — the Prophet's uncle who was one of his fiercest enemies — and his wife who spread thorns in the Prophet's path. It declares that his wealth and children will not save him.

Key Lessons

  • May the hands of Abu Lahab be ruined — and ruined is he (111:1).
  • His wealth will not avail him nor what he gained (111:2).
  • He will enter a blazing fire — and his wife, the carrier of firewood — with a rope of palm fibre around her neck (111:3-5).
