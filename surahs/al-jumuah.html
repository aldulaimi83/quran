Surah 62 — الجمعة

Surah Al-Jumu'ah — Friday

Read Surah Al-Jumu'ah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 62 of the Holy Quran — Friday — 11 verses.

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا نُودِيَ لِلصَّلَاةِ مِن يَوْمِ الْجُمُعَةِ

Simple Meaning

This surah commands believers to hasten to the Friday prayer when they hear the call to prayer, leaving their trade and business aside. It also rebukes those who left the Prophet during a khutbah for an arriving trade caravan.

Key Lessons

  • O you who believe, when the call to prayer is made on Friday, hasten to the remembrance of Allah and leave off trade (62:9).
  • Then when the prayer is completed, disperse through the land and seek from the bounty of Allah (62:10).
  • Say: What is with Allah is better than amusement and trade — and Allah is the Best of providers (62:11).
