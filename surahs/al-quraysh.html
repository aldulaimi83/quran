لِإِيلَافِ قُرَيْشٍ
إِيلَافِهِمْ رِحْلَةَ الشِّتَاءِ وَالصَّيْفِ
فَلْيَعْبُدُوا رَبَّ هَٰذَا الْبَيْتِ
الَّذِي أَطْعَمَهُم مِّن جُوعٍ وَآمَنَهُم مِّنْ خَوْفٍ

Simple Meaning

Allah gave Quraysh safety in travel and provision from hunger. They were commanded to worship the Lord of the Ka'bah.

Key Lessons

  • Food and safety are blessings from Allah.
  • Comfort should lead to gratitude and worship.
  • Remember Allah when life feels secure.
