Surah 92 — الليل

Surah Al-Layl — The Night

Read Surah Al-Layl online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 92 of the Holy Quran — The Night — 21 verses.

وَاللَّيْلِ إِذَا يَغْشَىٰ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with oaths by the night, the day, and the creation of male and female. It then describes two types of people — those who give, are mindful of Allah, and believe in goodness, versus those who are miserly, self-sufficient, and deny it.

Key Lessons

  • By the night as it covers — by the day as it reveals (92:1-2).
  • As for the one who gives and is mindful of Allah and believes in goodness — We will ease him toward ease (92:5-7).
  • Indeed your efforts are diverse (92:4).
