Surah Az-Zukhruf — The Ornaments of Gold

Read Surah Az-Zukhruf online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 43 of the Holy Quran — The Ornaments of Gold — 89 verses.

حم ۝ وَالْكِتَابِ الْمُبِينِ

Simple Meaning

This surah addresses those who are deceived by the glitter of gold and worldly luxury. It tells the stories of Ibrahim and Musa, the claims about Isa being divine, and ends with a vision of the believers in Paradise.

Key Lessons

  • Ha-Mim — by the clear Book (43:1-2).
  • Isa was only a servant upon whom We bestowed favour and made an example for the Children of Israel (43:59).
  • And the friends on that Day will be enemies to one another — except the righteous (43:67).
