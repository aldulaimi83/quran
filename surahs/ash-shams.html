Surah 91 — الشمس

Surah Ash-Shams — The Sun

Read Surah Ash-Shams online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 91 of the Holy Quran — The Sun — 15 verses.

وَالشَّمْسِ وَضُحَاهَا

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with eleven oaths — by the sun, the moon, the day, the night, the sky, the earth, and the soul — all leading to one conclusion: successful is the one who purifies their soul, and ruined is the one who corrupts it.

Key Lessons

  • By the sun and its brightness — by the moon when it follows it — by the day when it displays it (91:1-3).
  • Successful is the one who purifies it — ruined is the one who corrupts it (91:9-10).
  • Thamud rejected out of their arrogance — when the most wretched of them rose up (91:11-12).
