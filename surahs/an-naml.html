Surah An-Naml — The Ant

إِنَّهُ مِن سُلَيْمَانَ وَإِنَّهُ بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Simple Meaning

This surah tells the stories of Musa, Sulayman and the Queen of Sheba (Bilqis), Salih and the she-camel, and Lut. It contains the famous letter from Sulayman beginning with Bismillah and the ant's warning to her colony.

Key Lessons

  • Indeed it is from Sulayman and it begins with the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful (27:30).
  • Say: Only Allah knows what is hidden in the heavens and the earth (27:65).
  • Your Lord is not heedless of what you do (27:93).
