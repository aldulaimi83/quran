Surah 73 — المزمل

Surah Al-Muzzammil — The Enshrouded One

Read Surah Al-Muzzammil online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 73 of the Holy Quran — The Enshrouded One — 20 verses.

يَا أَيُّهَا الْمُزَّمِّلُ ۝ قُمِ اللَّيْلَ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا

Simple Meaning

This surah commands the Prophet to rise at night for prayer — half the night, or a little less or more. It emphasises that the night prayer strengthens the soul, and ends by urging the believers to pray, pay zakat, and give a beautiful loan to Allah.

Key Lessons

  • O you wrapped in garments — rise the night, except a little (73:1-2).
  • Truly the rising at night is most effective for the soul and most conducive to speech (73:6).
  • Establish prayer and give zakat — and whatever good you send ahead for your soul, you will find it with Allah (73:20).
