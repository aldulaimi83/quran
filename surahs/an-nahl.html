Surah 16 — النحل

Surah An-Nahl — The Bee

Read Surah An-Nahl online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 16 of the Holy Quran — The Bee — 128 verses.

وَأَوْحَىٰ رَبُّكَ إِلَى النَّحْلِ أَنِ اتَّخِذِي مِنَ الْجِبَالِ بُيُوتًا

Simple Meaning

This surah lists the countless blessings of Allah — cattle, ships, rain, fruits, honey — and calls people to be grateful. It contains the command to fulfil covenants and warns against breaking oaths after making them.

Key Lessons

  • Your Lord revealed to the bee: build homes in the mountains and in trees (16:68).
  • Verily, Allah commands justice, goodness, and giving to relatives (16:90).
  • Whoever does good deeds, whether male or female, and is a believer — We will give them a good life (16:97).
