Surah 65 — الطلاق

Surah At-Talaq — The Divorce

Read Surah At-Talaq online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 65 of the Holy Quran — The Divorce — 12 verses.

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ إِذَا طَلَّقْتُمُ النِّسَاءَ فَطَلِّقُوهُنَّ لِعِدَّتِهِنَّ

Simple Meaning

This surah contains detailed rulings about divorce, the waiting period ('iddah), and maintaining women well during that period. It ends with a reminder that Allah supports those who are mindful of Him and provides from where they do not expect.

Key Lessons

  • Whoever is mindful of Allah, He will make a way out for him and provide for him from where he does not expect (65:2-3).
  • And whoever puts their trust in Allah — He is sufficient for them (65:3).
  • Allah will make things easy for those who are mindful of Him (65:4).
