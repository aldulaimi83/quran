Surah 11 — هود

Surah Hud — Hud

وَإِلَى عَادٍ أَخَاهُمْ هُودًا قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ اعْبُدُوا اللَّهَ

Simple Meaning

This surah narrates the stories of Nuh, Hud, Salih, Ibrahim, Lut, Shu'ayb, and Musa. Each prophet called their nation to worship Allah alone. The surah warns that Allah's punishment is severe for those who persist in wrongdoing.

Key Lessons

  • To the people of 'Ad, Allah sent their brother Hud — he said: O my people, worship Allah (11:50).
  • Seek forgiveness from your Lord and turn to Him in repentance (11:3).
  • Put your trust in Allah — He is the Ever-Living who never dies (25:58).
