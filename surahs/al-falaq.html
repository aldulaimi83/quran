Surah 113 — الفلق

Surah Al-Falaq — The Daybreak

Read Surah Al-Falaq online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 113 of the Holy Quran — The Daybreak — 5 verses.

قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ الْفَلَقِ ۝ مِن شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ ۝ وَمِن شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ

Simple Meaning

One of the two protective surahs (Al-Mu'awwidhatayn) recited morning and evening. It seeks refuge in the Lord of the daybreak from every created evil, from the darkness of the night, from those who blow on knots, and from the envious eye.

Key Lessons

  • Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of the daybreak (113:1).
  • From the evil of that which He created (113:2).
  • And from the evil of the envier when he envies (113:5).
