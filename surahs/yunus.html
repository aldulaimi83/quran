Surah 10 — يونس

Surah Yunus — Jonah

Read Surah Yunus online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 10 of the Holy Quran — Jonah — 109 verses.

أَلَا إِنَّ أَوْلِيَاءَ اللَّهِ لَا خَوْفٌ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا هُمْ يَحْزَنُونَ

Simple Meaning

This surah tells of the story of Yunus (Jonah), who called his people and was swallowed by a whale. It addresses the signs of Allah in creation, the falseness of the gods people invent, and the mercy of repentance.

Key Lessons

  • The friends of Allah — there is no fear for them, nor shall they grieve (10:62).
  • Say: It is from the grace of Allah and His mercy — in that they should rejoice (10:58).
  • Yunus called from the darkness: There is no god but You — glory be to You — I was among the wrongdoers (21:87).
