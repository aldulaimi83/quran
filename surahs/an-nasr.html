Surah 110 — النصر

Surah An-Nasr — The Divine Support

Read Surah An-Nasr online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 110 of the Holy Quran — The Divine Support — 3 verses.

إِذَا جَاءَ نَصْرُ اللَّهِ وَالْفَتْحُ ۝ وَرَأَيْتَ النَّاسَ يَدْخُلُونَ فِي دِينِ اللَّهِ أَفْوَاجًا

Simple Meaning

Revealed near the end of the Prophet's life, this surah tells of the coming of Allah's help and the conquest of Makkah, when people will enter the religion of Allah in multitudes. It commands the Prophet to glorify and seek forgiveness.

Key Lessons

  • When the help of Allah comes and the conquest (110:1).
  • And you see people entering the religion of Allah in multitudes (110:2).
  • Glorify the praise of your Lord and seek His forgiveness — truly He is Ever-Relenting (110:3).
