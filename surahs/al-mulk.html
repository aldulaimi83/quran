Surah 67 — الملك

Surah Al-Mulk — The Sovereignty

Read Surah Al-Mulk online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 67 of the Holy Quran — The Sovereignty — 30 verses.

تَبَارَكَ الَّذِي بِيَدِهِ الْمُلْكُ وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

Simple Meaning

Recommended to recite every night, Al-Mulk intercedes for its reciter on the Day of Judgment. It speaks of death and life as a test, the seven heavens, and warns those who think they are safe from Allah's punishment.

Key Lessons

  • Blessed is He in whose Hand is all sovereignty — and He has power over all things (67:1).
  • He who created death and life to test which of you is best in deeds (67:2).
  • Recite Al-Mulk every night — it intercedes for its reciter until it is forgiven (hadith).
