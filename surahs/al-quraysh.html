Surah 106

Surah Quraysh Meaning and Tafsir

Surah Quraysh reminds the people of Makkah that security, food, and successful travel are blessings from Allah. This page gives the meaning in English and simple tafsir.

Arabic Text

لِإِيلَافِ قُرَيْشٍ
إِيلَافِهِمْ رِحْلَةَ الشِّتَاءِ وَالصَّيْفِ
فَلْيَعْبُدُوا رَبَّ هَٰذَا الْبَيْتِ
الَّذِي أَطْعَمَهُم مِّن جُوعٍ وَآمَنَهُم مِّنْ خَوْفٍ

English Translation

Allah gave Quraysh security in their journeys and provision from hunger, so they were commanded to worship the Lord of the Ka'bah who protected and provided for them.

Word-by-Word Meaning

Ilaf means familiarity or peaceful arrangement. Rihlah means journey. Amanahum means He made them safe.

Tafsir (Simple Explanation)

The surah connects comfort with gratitude. When Allah gives a community stability, food, and peace, the right response is worship, not forgetfulness.

Benefits of Surah Quraysh

  • It teaches gratitude for simple blessings like food and safety.
  • It reminds believers to connect worldly comfort with worship.
  • It is a short surah suitable for new learners.

Explanation for Kids

Surah Quraysh teaches children that when Allah gives us food and safety, we should thank Him and obey Him. Blessings should make us grateful, not careless.
