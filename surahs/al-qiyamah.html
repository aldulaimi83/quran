Surah 75 — القيامة

Surah Al-Qiyamah — The Resurrection

Read Surah Al-Qiyamah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 75 of the Holy Quran — The Resurrection — 40 verses.

لَا أُقْسِمُ بِيَوْمِ الْقِيَامَةِ

Simple Meaning

This surah affirms the resurrection through an oath by the Day of Judgment and the self-reproaching soul. It describes the terror of the death moment and asks: does man think he will be left without account? It also promises that Allah will explain the Quran.

Key Lessons

  • I swear by the Day of Resurrection (75:1).
  • Does man think that he will be left without account? (75:36).
  • Upon Us is its collection and its recitation — and when We recite it, follow its recitation (75:17-18).
