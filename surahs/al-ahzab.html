Surah 33 — الأحزاب

Surah Al-Ahzab — The Combined Forces

Read Surah Al-Ahzab online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 33 of the Holy Quran — The Combined Forces — 73 verses.

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ اتَّقِ اللَّهَ وَلَا تُطِعِ الْكَافِرِينَ

Simple Meaning

This surah addresses events around the Battle of the Trench — the coalition of tribes that besieged Madinah. It also discusses the duties of believers, the status of the Prophet's wives, and the command to send salawat upon the Prophet.

Key Lessons

  • Indeed Allah and His angels send blessings on the Prophet — O believers, send blessings on him too (33:56).
  • Indeed in the Messenger of Allah you have a beautiful example (33:21).
  • We offered the trust to the heavens, the earth, and the mountains — they refused to bear it, but man took it on (33:72).
