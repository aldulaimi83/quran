Surah 69 — الحاقة

Surah Al-Haqqah — The Inevitable Reality

Read Surah Al-Haqqah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 69 of the Holy Quran — The Inevitable Reality — 52 verses.

الْحَاقَّةُ ۝ مَا الْحَاقَّةُ ۝ وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا الْحَاقَّةُ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with the Inevitable Reality — the Day of Judgment — and describes how previous nations like 'Ad, Thamud, Pharaoh, and the cities of Lut were destroyed. It then describes the blowing of the trumpet and the scene on Judgment Day.

Key Lessons

  • The Inevitable Reality — what is the Inevitable Reality? And what will make you realise what the Inevitable Reality is? (69:1-3).
  • As for one who is given his record in his right hand — he will say: Here, read my record! (69:19).
  • Truly this is the word of a noble Messenger (69:40).
