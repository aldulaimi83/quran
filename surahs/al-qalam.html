Surah 68 — القلم

Surah Al-Qalam — The Pen

Read Surah Al-Qalam online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 68 of the Holy Quran — The Pen — 52 verses.

ن وَالْقَلَمِ وَمَا يَسْطُرُونَ

Simple Meaning

Beginning with the letter Nun and an oath by the pen, this surah defends the Prophet's character against accusations of madness. It tells the parable of the owners of the garden who refused to give the poor their share and were destroyed overnight.

Key Lessons

  • Nun — by the pen and what they write (68:1).
  • You are surely of a great and noble character (68:4).
  • Verily for those who are mindful of their Lord, there are Gardens of Delight (68:34).
