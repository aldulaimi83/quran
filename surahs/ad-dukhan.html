Surah 44 — الدخان

Surah Ad-Dukhan — The Smoke

Read Surah Ad-Dukhan online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 44 of the Holy Quran — The Smoke — 59 verses.

حم ۝ وَالْكِتَابِ الْمُبِينِ ۝ إِنَّا أَنزَلْنَاهُ فِي لَيْلَةٍ مُّبَارَكَةٍ

Simple Meaning

This surah mentions the Night of Decree when the Quran was revealed, warns of a coming smoke as a sign of punishment, and tells of the fate of Pharaoh. It ends with a scene of the tree of Zaqqum in Hell and the blessings of Paradise.

Key Lessons

  • Ha-Mim — by the clear Book — We sent it down on a blessed night (44:1-3).
  • Were they created without any purpose? Or are they the creators? (52:35).
  • Indeed We sent it down in the Night of Decree (97:1).
