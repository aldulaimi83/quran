Surah 6 — الأنعام

Surah Al-An'am — The Cattle

Read Surah Al-An'am online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 6 of the Holy Quran — The Cattle — 165 verses.

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ

Simple Meaning

This Meccan surah addresses the oneness of Allah, the rejection of polytheism, and the prophethood of Ibrahim. It contains the six fundamentals of belief and strong arguments against those who deny resurrection.

Key Lessons

  • All praise belongs to Allah who created the heavens and the earth (6:1).
  • Say: My prayer, my sacrifice, my life and my death are all for Allah (6:162).
  • Do not follow the footsteps of Shaytan — he is your clear enemy (6:142).
