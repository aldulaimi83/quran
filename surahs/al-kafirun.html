Surah 109

Surah Al-Kafirun - Translation and Tafsir

Surah Al-Kafirun teaches clarity in worship and sincere dedication to Allah alone.

قُلْ يَا أَيُّهَا الْكَافِرُونَ
لَا أَعْبُدُ مَا تَعْبُدُونَ
وَلَا أَنتُمْ عَابِدُونَ مَا أَعْبُدُ
وَلَا أَنَا عَابِدٌ مَّا عَبَدتُّمْ
وَلَا أَنتُمْ عَابِدُونَ مَا أَعْبُدُ
لَكُمْ دِينُكُمْ وَلِيَ دِينِ

Simple Meaning

This surah makes clear that Muslims worship Allah alone and do not mix that worship with false beliefs.

Key Lessons

  • Worship must be sincere and clear.
  • Faith should not be compromised.
  • Respectful clarity is better than confusion.
