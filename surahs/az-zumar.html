Surah 39 — الزمر

Surah Az-Zumar — The Troops

Read Surah Az-Zumar online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 39 of the Holy Quran — The Troops — 75 verses.

أَلَا لِلَّهِ الدِّينُ الْخَالِصُ

Simple Meaning

This surah emphasises that sincere religion is for Allah alone. It contains the famous verse of hope — that Allah forgives all sins — and concludes with a vivid scene of the afterlife where the wrongdoers are herded in groups to Hell and the righteous to Paradise.

Key Lessons

  • Say: O My servants who have wronged themselves — do not despair of Allah's mercy. Truly Allah forgives all sins (39:53).
  • Allah descends the best speech — a Book that is consistent and repeated (39:23).
  • And the earth will shine with the light of its Lord on the Day of Judgment (39:69).
