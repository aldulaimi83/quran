Surah 14 — إبراهيم

Surah Ibrahim — Abraham

رَبِّ اجْعَلْنِي مُقِيمَ الصَّلَاةِ وَمِن ذُرِّيَّتِي

Simple Meaning

This surah contains the beautiful duas of Ibrahim for his family and his city of Makkah. It speaks of the blessings of Allah that cannot be counted and warns of the Day when every wrongdoer will be punished.

Key Lessons

  • My Lord, make me one who establishes prayer, and from my descendants (14:40).
  • If you are grateful, I will surely increase you in blessing (14:7).
  • A good word is like a good tree — its root is firm and its branches reach the sky (14:24).
