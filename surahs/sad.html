ص وَالْقُرْآنِ ذِي الذِّكْرِ

Simple Meaning

This surah tells of Dawud and his judgment, Sulayman's devotion, and Ayyub's patience in suffering. It contains the dialogue of Iblis with Allah where Iblis refuses to bow and vows to mislead humans until Judgment Day.

Key Lessons

  • Sad — by the Quran full of reminder (38:1).
  • Iblis said: By Your might, I will mislead them all — except Your sincere servants (38:82-83).
  • And remember Our servant Ayyub — when he called his Lord: Adversity has touched me, and You are the Most Merciful (21:83).
