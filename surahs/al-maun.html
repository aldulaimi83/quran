Surah 107 — الماعون

Surah Al-Ma'un — The Small Kindnesses

Read Surah Al-Ma'un online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 107 of the Holy Quran — The Small Kindnesses — 7 verses.

أَرَأَيْتَ الَّذِي يُكَذِّبُ بِالدِّينِ ۝ فَذَٰلِكَ الَّذِي يَدُعُّ الْيَتِيمَ

Simple Meaning

This surah defines true faith through action. The one who denies the religion is the one who pushes away the orphan and does not encourage feeding the poor. It also warns those who pray but are heedless and refuse small acts of kindness.

Key Lessons

  • Have you seen the one who denies the religion? That is the one who pushes away the orphan (107:1-2).
  • And does not encourage the feeding of the poor (107:3).
  • Woe to those who pray but are heedless of their prayer — those who make a show but refuse small kindnesses (107:4-7).
