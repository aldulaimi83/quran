Surah 95 — التين

Surah At-Tin — The Fig

Read Surah At-Tin online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 95 of the Holy Quran — The Fig — 8 verses.

وَالتِّينِ وَالزَّيْتُونِ ۝ وَطُورِ سِينِينَ ۝ وَهَٰذَا الْبَلَدِ الْأَمِينِ

Simple Meaning

This short surah swears by the fig, the olive, Mount Sinai, and the safe city (Makkah). It declares that Allah created man in the best form but those who reject faith and do not do good deeds will be brought to the lowest level.

Key Lessons

  • By the fig and the olive — by Mount Sinai — by this secure city (95:1-3).
  • We have certainly created man in the best of moulds (95:4).
  • Then We reduced him to the lowest of the low — except those who believe and do righteous deeds (95:5-6).
