Surah 74 — المدثر

Surah Al-Muddaththir — The Cloaked One

Read Surah Al-Muddaththir online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 74 of the Holy Quran — The Cloaked One — 56 verses.

يَا أَيُّهَا الْمُدَّثِّرُ ۝ قُمْ فَأَنذِرْ

Simple Meaning

Among the earliest revelations, this surah calls the Prophet to rise and warn. It speaks of the fate of those who rejected the Quran, and the inmates of the Fire who admit they neglected prayer and the poor.

Key Lessons

  • O you who is wrapped up — arise and warn (74:1-2).
  • And they will say: We were not of those who prayed, and we did not feed the poor (74:43-44).
  • Nay — this is surely a reminder — so let whoever wills take heed of it (74:54-55).
