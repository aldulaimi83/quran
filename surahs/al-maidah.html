Surah 5 — المائدة

Surah Al-Ma'idah — The Table Spread

الْيَوْمَ أَكْمَلْتُ لَكُمْ دِينَكُمْ وَأَتْمَمْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ نِعْمَتِي

Simple Meaning

This surah discusses food laws, contracts, justice, the punishment of thieves, and the Christian and Jewish beliefs about Isa (Jesus). It contains the famous verse declaring that Islam was completed as a religion.

Key Lessons

  • Today I have perfected your religion for you and completed My favour upon you (5:3).
  • Whoever saves one life, it is as if he has saved all of mankind (5:32).
  • O you who believe, fulfil your contracts (5:1).
