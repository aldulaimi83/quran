Surah 49 — الحجرات

Surah Al-Hujurat — The Rooms

Read Surah Al-Hujurat online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 49 of the Holy Quran — The Rooms — 18 verses.

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تُقَدِّمُوا بَيْنَ يَدَيِ اللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِ

Simple Meaning

This surah is a manual of Islamic manners — do not raise your voices above the Prophet's, verify news before acting on it, do not mock one another, avoid suspicion and spying, and do not backbite. It ends by explaining the difference between Islam and true faith.

Key Lessons

  • O believers, if an evildoer brings you news, verify it (49:6).
  • O people, We created you from male and female and made you into nations — the most noble of you in the sight of Allah is the most righteous (49:13).
  • Do not spy on each other, and do not backbite one another (49:12).
