Surah 72 — الجن

Surah Al-Jinn — The Jinn

Read Surah Al-Jinn online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 72 of the Holy Quran — The Jinn — 28 verses.

قُلْ أُوحِيَ إِلَيَّ أَنَّهُ اسْتَمَعَ نَفَرٌ مِّنَ الْجِنِّ

Simple Meaning

This surah reveals the speech of a group of jinn who listened to the Quran and believed. They returned to their people with the message. It reminds that the mosques belong to Allah and that only Allah knows the unseen.

Key Lessons

  • Say: It has been revealed to me that a group of jinn listened (to the Quran) and said: We have heard an amazing recitation (72:1).
  • And the mosques are for Allah, so do not invoke anyone along with Allah (72:18).
  • He knows the unseen and does not reveal it to anyone except a messenger He has approved (72:26-27).
