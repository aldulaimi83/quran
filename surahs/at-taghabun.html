Surah 64 — التغابن

Surah At-Taghabun — Mutual Disillusion

Read Surah At-Taghabun online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 64 of the Holy Quran — Mutual Disillusion — 18 verses.

يُسَبِّحُ لِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ

Simple Meaning

This surah reminds people that the Day of Judgment is the Day of Mutual Disillusion — where people will realise the truth too late. It calls believers to spend in the path of Allah and warns that wealth and children can be a trial.

Key Lessons

  • The Day He gathers you for the Day of Gathering — that is the Day of Mutual Disillusion (64:9).
  • Indeed your wealth and your children are a trial for you — but with Allah is a great reward (64:15).
  • No disaster strikes except by the permission of Allah — and whoever believes in Allah, He will guide his heart (64:11).
