Surah 100 — العاديات

Surah Al-Adiyat — The Coursers

Read Surah Al-Adiyat online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 100 of the Holy Quran — The Coursers — 11 verses.

وَالْعَادِيَاتِ ضَبْحًا

Simple Meaning

Opening with oaths by charging warhorses that strike sparks and raise clouds of dust at dawn, this surah points out man's ingratitude to his Lord and his love for wealth. It then describes the graves being overturned on Judgment Day.

Key Lessons

  • By the charging horses panting — by those who strike sparks with their hooves (100:1-2).
  • Truly man is ungrateful to his Lord — and to that he bears witness (100:6-7).
  • Does he not know — when the graves are overturned — and what is in the chests is made known? (100:9-10).
