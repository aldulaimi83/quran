Surah 86 — الطارق

Surah At-Tariq — The Morning Star

Read Surah At-Tariq online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 86 of the Holy Quran — The Morning Star — 17 verses.

وَالسَّمَاءِ وَالطَّارِقِ

Simple Meaning

Beginning with an oath by the piercing star that appears by night, this surah reminds man of how he was created from a gushing fluid. It warns that on the Day of Judgment, man will have no power and no helper — it is the Day of decision.

Key Lessons

  • By the sky and by the night visitor — and what will explain to you what the night visitor is? — the piercing star! (86:1-3).
  • Man should consider what he was created from — he was created from a gushing fluid (86:5-6).
  • Indeed the Quran is a decisive word — it is not a joke (86:13-14).
