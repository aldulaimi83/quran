Surah 89 — الفجر

Surah Al-Fajr — The Dawn

Read Surah Al-Fajr online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 89 of the Holy Quran — The Dawn — 30 verses.

وَالْفَجْرِ ۝ وَلَيَالٍ عَشْرٍ

Simple Meaning

Opening with oaths by the dawn and ten nights, this surah speaks of the destroyed nations of 'Ad, Thamud, and Pharaoh. It addresses man's ingratitude — boasting when blessed and despairing when tested. It ends with the beautiful call to the peaceful soul.

Key Lessons

  • By the dawn — by the ten nights (89:1-2).
  • O reassured soul — return to your Lord pleased and pleasing (89:27-28).
  • And you do not honour the orphan, nor encourage feeding the poor (89:17-18).
