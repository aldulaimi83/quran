Surah 15 — الحجر

Surah Al-Hijr — The Rocky Tract

وَلَقَدْ جَعَلْنَا فِي السَّمَاءِ بُرُوجًا

Simple Meaning

This surah addresses the story of Ibrahim and Lut, the creation of man from clay, and Iblis's refusal to prostrate. It reminds the Prophet that the Quran is preserved under Allah's protection.

Key Lessons

  • Truly, it is We who sent down the Reminder and it is We who will preserve it (15:9).
  • Iblis refused and was arrogant — he became one of the disbelievers (2:34).
  • Do not grieve over them and lower your wing with mercy for the believers (15:88).
