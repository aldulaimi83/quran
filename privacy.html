Privacy Policy

This page explains the basic privacy practices used on Quran YouOOO.

What this site does

Quran YouOOO provides a browser-based Quran reading experience with translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word support.

Analytics

The site uses website analytics to understand traffic and improve performance and usability. These analytics are used for general measurement, not for selling personal information.

Reader preferences

The Quran reader stores some preferences in your browser, such as theme and study settings, so the site can remember your reading experience on the same device.

Third-party services

Quran content, recitation, translation, and tafsir data may rely on public Quran-related APIs or content services used by the existing reader. Their availability and policies may affect how content is delivered.

Contact

If you have a privacy question about this website, use the contact page for the available contact options.
