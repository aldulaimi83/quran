Surah 81 — التكوير

Surah At-Takwir — The Overthrowing

Read Surah At-Takwir online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 81 of the Holy Quran — The Overthrowing — 29 verses.

إِذَا الشَّمْسُ كُوِّرَتْ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with a rapid series of cosmic events on the Last Day: the sun dimmed, stars fallen, mountains moved, oceans set ablaze. It then affirms that the Quran is the word of a noble angel and is not the speech of a madman.

Key Lessons

  • When the sun is wrapped up (81:1).
  • Then every soul will know what it has brought (81:14).
  • Truly this is the word of a noble messenger — endowed with power, secure with the Lord of the Throne (81:19-20).
