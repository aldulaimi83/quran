Surah 99 — الزلزلة

Surah Az-Zalzalah — The Earthquake

Read Surah Az-Zalzalah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 99 of the Holy Quran — The Earthquake — 8 verses.

إِذَا زُلْزِلَتِ الْأَرْضُ زِلْزَالَهَا ۝ وَأَخْرَجَتِ الْأَرْضُ أَثْقَالَهَا

Simple Meaning

This short, powerful surah describes the great earthquake of Judgment Day — when the earth is shaken with its final quaking, throws out its burdens, and man asks what is wrong with it. The earth will then speak, testifying to every deed done on it.

Key Lessons

  • When the earth is shaken with its final earthquake — and the earth throws out its burdens (99:1-2).
  • That Day it will report its news (99:4).
  • Whoever does an atom's weight of good will see it — and whoever does an atom's weight of evil will see it (99:7-8).
