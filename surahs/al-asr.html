Surah 103 — العصر

Surah Al-Asr — The Declining Day

Read Surah Al-Asr online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 103 of the Holy Quran — The Declining Day — 3 verses.

وَالْعَصْرِ ۝ إِنَّ الْإِنسَانَ لَفِي خُسْرٍ ۝ إِلَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالْحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالصَّبْرِ

Simple Meaning

Despite being only three verses, Al-Asr contains the entire formula for success in life. Imam Shafi'i said: If people reflected on this surah alone, it would be sufficient for them. Every human is in loss — except those who believe, do good, and encourage each other in truth and patience.

Key Lessons

  • By time — indeed mankind is in loss (103:1-2).
  • Except those who believe, do righteous deeds, and encourage each other to truth and patience (103:3).
  • Imam Shafi'i said: If this were the only surah revealed, it would be sufficient guidance for all of mankind.
