قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ الْفَلَقِ
مِن شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ
وَمِن شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ
وَمِن شَرِّ النَّفَّاثَاتِ فِي الْعُقَدِ
وَمِن شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ

Simple Meaning

This surah asks Allah to protect us from harmful things, darkness, envy, and hidden evil. It reminds us that true safety comes from Allah.

Key Lessons

  • Ask Allah for protection in the morning and evening.
  • Do not fear creation more than the Creator.
  • Protect the heart from envy and harm.
