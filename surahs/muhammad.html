Surah 47 — محمد

Surah Muhammad — Muhammad

الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ وَآمَنُوا بِمَا نُزِّلَ عَلَىٰ مُحَمَّدٍ

Simple Meaning

Also called Surah Al-Qital (The Fighting), this surah was revealed around the time of Badr and speaks of the battle against the disbelievers. It warns against following one's desires and calls believers to steadfastness and not to weaken.

Key Lessons

  • Do not weaken and do not call for peace when you have the upper hand — Allah is with you and will not deprive you of your deeds (47:35).
  • Those who disbelieve — their deeds are like ashes that the wind scatters on a stormy day (14:18).
  • Know that there is no god but Allah and seek forgiveness for your sin (47:19).
