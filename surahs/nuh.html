Surah 71 — نوح

Surah Nuh — Noah

Read Surah Nuh online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 71 of the Holy Quran — Noah — 28 verses.

إِنَّا أَرْسَلْنَا نُوحًا إِلَىٰ قَوْمِهِ

Simple Meaning

This surah tells the story of Nuh (Noah) entirely from his own perspective — his long years of calling, his people's stubborn rejection, his final prayer, and the command to build the ark. It ends with Nuh's beautiful supplication.

Key Lessons

  • We sent Nuh to his people — calling them for a thousand years less fifty (29:14).
  • Nuh said: My Lord, I called my people night and day — but my call only made them flee further (71:5-6).
  • My Lord, forgive me and my parents and all the believers on the Day of Reckoning (71:28).
