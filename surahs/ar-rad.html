Surah 13 — الرعد

Surah Ar-Ra'd — The Thunder

اللَّهُ يَعْلَمُ مَا تَحْمِلُ كُلُّ أُنثَىٰ

Simple Meaning

This surah speaks of the signs of Allah in creation — thunder, lightning, rain, and the earth. It emphasises that real change must come from within, and that Allah alone has knowledge of the unseen.

Key Lessons

  • Indeed, Allah does not change the condition of a people until they change what is in themselves (13:11).
  • Verily in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find rest (13:28).
  • The thunder glorifies His praise, as do the angels in awe of Him (13:13).
