Surah 22 — الحج

Surah Al-Hajj — The Pilgrimage

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ اتَّقُوا رَبَّكُمْ إِنَّ زَلْزَلَةَ السَّاعَةِ شَيْءٌ عَظِيمٌ

Simple Meaning

This surah describes the Day of Judgment with vivid intensity, then speaks of the Hajj pilgrimage and its rites. It declares that it is not the blood of sacrificial animals that reaches Allah but the taqwa from the heart.

Key Lessons

  • It is not their flesh nor blood that reaches Allah — it is your taqwa that reaches Him (22:37).
  • Allah will defend those who believe — indeed Allah does not love every treacherous ingrate (22:38).
  • Proclaim the Hajj to the people — they will come to you on foot and on every lean camel (22:27).
