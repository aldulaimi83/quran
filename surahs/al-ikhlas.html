Surah Al-Ikhlas - Translation, Tafsir, Audio & Word-by-Word Learning

Read Surah Al-Ikhlas online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word by word meaning about the oneness of Allah. This page is designed to help readers listen Quran online, understand Quran with translation, and study Quran with tafsir in a simple format.

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ
اللَّهُ الصَّمَدُ
لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ
وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ

Simple Meaning

Allah is One, complete, and independent. He has no children, no parents, and nothing is equal or comparable to Him.

Key Lessons

  • Allah alone deserves worship.
  • Allah is perfect and does not need anyone.
  • True faith starts with knowing Allah correctly.
