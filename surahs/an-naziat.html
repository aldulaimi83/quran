Surah 79 — النازعات

Surah An-Nazi'at — Those Who Drag Forth

Read Surah An-Nazi'at online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 79 of the Holy Quran — Those Who Drag Forth — 46 verses.

وَالنَّازِعَاتِ غَرْقًا

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with oaths by the angels who pull out souls and carry out commands. It tells of Musa being sent to Pharaoh, Pharaoh's arrogance and denial, and his destruction. It then describes the Day of Judgment and the two groups.

Key Lessons

  • By those who pull out souls violently — by those who take them out gently (79:1-2).
  • Go to Pharaoh, for he has transgressed — and speak to him gently, perhaps he will be reminded or fear (20:43-44).
  • As for those who feared the standing before their Lord and restrained themselves from desires — Paradise will be their shelter (79:40-41).
