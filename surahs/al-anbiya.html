Surah 21 — الأنبياء

Surah Al-Anbiya — The Prophets

Read Surah Al-Anbiya online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 21 of the Holy Quran — The Prophets — 112 verses.

وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَا قَبْلَكَ إِلَّا رِجَالًا نُوحِي إِلَيْهِمْ

Simple Meaning

This surah mentions eighteen prophets and their stories — Ibrahim, Lut, Nuh, Dawud, Sulayman, Ayyub, Yunus, Zakariya, Yahya, and others. It emphasises that all prophets were human beings who received revelation.

Key Lessons

  • We did not send before you except men to whom We revealed — so ask the people of knowledge if you do not know (21:7).
  • And Dhul-Nun called out in the darkness: There is no god but You — glory be to You — I was among the wrongdoers (21:87).
  • We have written in the Psalms that the earth shall be inherited by My righteous servants (21:105).
