Surah 105 — الفيل

Surah Al-Fil — The Elephant

Read Surah Al-Fil online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 105 of the Holy Quran — The Elephant — 5 verses.

أَلَمْ تَرَ كَيْفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِأَصْحَابِ الْفِيلِ

Simple Meaning

This surah refers to the Year of the Elephant — when Abraha came with elephants to destroy the Kaaba. Allah sent birds carrying stones of baked clay that destroyed the army, leaving them like eaten straw.

Key Lessons

  • Have you not considered how your Lord dealt with the companions of the elephant? (105:1).
  • Did He not make their plan go astray? (105:2).
  • He sent against them birds in flocks — striking them with stones of baked clay — leaving them like eaten straw (105:3-5).
