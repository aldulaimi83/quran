Surah 57 — الحديد

Surah Al-Hadid — The Iron

Read Surah Al-Hadid online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 57 of the Holy Quran — The Iron — 29 verses.

سَبَّحَ لِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with all things glorifying Allah. It speaks of the light given to the believers on the Day of Judgment and calls people to spend in Allah's path. It reminds us that worldly life is like rain that makes plants grow then wither.

Key Lessons

  • Know that the life of this world is but amusement and diversion — the worldly life is only the enjoyment of delusion (57:20).
  • Spend in the cause of Allah and do not cast yourselves into destruction (2:195).
  • He is the First and the Last, the Manifest and the Hidden — and He has knowledge of all things (57:3).
