Surah 58 — المجادلة

Surah Al-Mujadila — The Pleading Woman

Read Surah Al-Mujadila online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 58 of the Holy Quran — The Pleading Woman — 22 verses.

قَدْ سَمِعَ اللَّهُ قَوْلَ الَّتِي تُجَادِلُكَ فِي زَوْجِهَا

Simple Meaning

This surah begins with the story of a woman who came to the Prophet about her husband's wrongful oath of zihar. Allah heard her complaint and responded. It also addresses private consultations and the manners of gatherings.

Key Lessons

  • Allah has certainly heard the argument of the woman who disputed with you about her husband (58:1).
  • Allah will raise those who believe among you and those given knowledge by degrees (58:11).
  • You will not find a people who believe in Allah and the Last Day loving those who oppose Allah (58:22).
