Surah 52 — الطور

Surah At-Tur — The Mount

Read Surah At-Tur online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 52 of the Holy Quran — The Mount — 49 verses.

وَالطُّورِ ۝ وَكِتَابٍ مَّسْطُورٍ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with oaths by the Mount (Sinai), the written book, the inhabited house, the raised ceiling, and the full sea. It then describes the Day of Judgment, the reward of the righteous, and calls on the Prophet to be patient.

Key Lessons

  • By the Mount, and a written book (52:1-2).
  • So wait patiently for the judgment of your Lord — you are in Our sight (52:48).
  • And the believing children who followed them will be joined with them in Paradise (52:21).
