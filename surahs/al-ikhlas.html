Surah 112 — الإخلاص

Surah Al-Ikhlas — The Sincerity

Read Surah Al-Ikhlas online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 112 of the Holy Quran — The Sincerity — 4 verses.

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ ۝ اللَّهُ الصَّمَدُ ۝ لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ ۝ وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ

Simple Meaning

Equal to one third of the Quran in reward, Al-Ikhlas declares the pure oneness of Allah in four verses. Allah is One, the Self-Sufficient, the Eternal. He neither begets nor was begotten, and there is nothing comparable to Him.

Key Lessons

  • Say: He is Allah, the One (112:1).
  • Allah, the Eternal, the Absolute (112:2).
  • He neither begets nor was begotten — and there is nothing comparable to Him (112:3-4).
