Surah An-Nasr - Translation, Tafsir, Audio & Word-by-Word Learning

Read Surah An-Nasr online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word by word meaning about victory, praise, and forgiveness. This page is designed to help readers listen Quran online, understand Quran with translation, and study Quran with tafsir in a simple format.

إِذَا جَاءَ نَصْرُ اللَّهِ وَالْفَتْحُ
وَرَأَيْتَ النَّاسَ يَدْخُلُونَ فِي دِينِ اللَّهِ أَفْوَاجًا
فَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ وَاسْتَغْفِرْهُ إِنَّهُ كَانَ تَوَّابًا

Simple Meaning

When Allah gives victory and people enter Islam, believers should praise Allah and seek His forgiveness.

Key Lessons

  • Success is from Allah, not from pride.
  • Thank Allah when good things happen.
  • Seeking forgiveness is always needed.
