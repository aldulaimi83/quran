Surah 80 — عبس

Surah Abasa — He Frowned

Read Surah Abasa online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 80 of the Holy Quran — He Frowned — 42 verses.

عَبَسَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ ۝ أَن جَاءَهُ الْأَعْمَىٰ

Simple Meaning

This surah gently rebukes the Prophet for frowning at a blind man (Ibn Umm Maktum) while giving attention to a wealthy leader. It teaches that every person who comes seeking guidance deserves equal care and respect.

Key Lessons

  • He frowned and turned away because the blind man came to him (80:1-2).
  • As for the one who came to you striving and with awe of Allah — you were distracted from him (80:8-10).
  • O man, what has deceived you concerning your Most Generous Lord? (82:6).
