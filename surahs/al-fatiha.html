Surah Al-Fatiha Full Explanation Simple (Meaning in English)

Surah Al-Fatiha is the opening chapter of the Quran and the most repeated surah in daily prayer. This page explains the meaning in English, simple tafsir, benefits, and easy lessons for children and beginners.

Arabic Text

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ
إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ
اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ
صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّالِّينَ

English Translation

Surah Al-Fatiha begins with Allah's mercy, praises Him as the Lord of all worlds, and teaches believers to ask only Him for help and guidance. It is a prayer for the straight path every Muslim repeats in salah.

Word-by-Word Meaning

Al-hamdu lillah means all praise belongs to Allah. Rabb al-alamin means Lord of all worlds. Iyyaka na'budu means You alone we worship. Ihdina as-sirat al-mustaqim means guide us to the straight path.

Tafsir (Simple Explanation)

Al-Fatiha is a complete summary of a believer's relationship with Allah. It begins with praise, then reminds the heart of mercy and judgment, then moves into worship, dependence, and dua. The surah teaches that the right path is a path of guidance, gratitude, and obedience.

Benefits of Surah Al-Fatiha

  • It is read in every unit of salah, so it keeps the heart connected to worship every day.
  • It teaches praise, reliance, and dua in a short but powerful form.
  • It is one of the easiest and most important surahs for every Muslim to memorize and understand.

Explanation for Kids

Surah Al-Fatiha teaches children to love Allah, thank Him, and ask Him for help. It is like a daily prayer that says: Allah, You are kind, You are powerful, and please guide me to do what is right.
