Surah 7 — الأعراف

Surah Al-A'raf — The Heights

Read Surah Al-A'raf online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 7 of the Holy Quran — The Heights — 206 verses.

كِتَابٌ أُنْزِلَ إِلَيْكَ فَلَا يَكُنْ فِي صَدْرِكَ حَرَجٌ مِنْهُ

Simple Meaning

This surah tells the stories of Adam and Iblis, Nuh, Hud, Salih, Lut, Shu'ayb, and Musa. It warns of Shaytan's deception and reminds people of how previous nations were destroyed for their pride and rejection of truth.

Key Lessons

  • Shaytan said: I will come to them from before and behind, from their right and left (7:17).
  • Call upon your Lord in humility and in private — He does not love transgressors (7:55).
  • Those who believe and do righteous deeds — We do not burden any soul beyond its capacity (7:42).
