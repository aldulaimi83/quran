Surah 55

Surah Ar-Rahman Full Tafsir English

Surah Ar-Rahman is one of the most beloved chapters of the Quran because of its repeated reminder: which of your Lord's favors will you deny? This page gives a simple English tafsir and summary.

Arabic Text

الرَّحْمَٰنُ ... فَبِأَيِّ آلَاءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

English Translation

Surah Ar-Rahman highlights Allah's mercy through revelation, creation, order in the universe, human life, and the rewards and warnings of the hereafter.

Word-by-Word Meaning

Ar-Rahman means The Most Merciful. Ala' means favors, signs, or blessings. The repeated verse asks creation to reflect honestly on Allah's countless gifts.

Tafsir (Simple Explanation)

The surah is built around gratitude. It moves from the teaching of the Quran to the design of the world, then to judgment and paradise. The repetition is deliberate: it pushes the heart to stop denying what is obvious.

Benefits of Studying Surah Ar-Rahman

  • It increases gratitude and reflection.
  • It reminds readers that mercy and accountability go together.
  • It is a strong surah for teaching the signs of Allah in creation.

Explanation for Kids

For kids, Surah Ar-Rahman can be explained as a chapter that keeps asking us to notice Allah's gifts: the Quran, the sun, the moon, food, life, and the beauty of paradise.
