Surah An-Nas - Translation, Tafsir, Audio & Word-by-Word Learning

Read Surah An-Nas online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word by word meaning about protection from whispers. This page is designed to help readers listen Quran online, understand Quran with translation, and study Quran with tafsir in a simple format.

قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ النَّاسِ
مَلِكِ النَّاسِ
إِلَٰهِ النَّاسِ
مِن شَرِّ الْوَسْوَاسِ الْخَنَّاسِ
الَّذِي يُوَسْوِسُ فِي صُدُورِ النَّاسِ
مِنَ الْجِنَّةِ وَالنَّاسِ

Simple Meaning

Allah is the Lord, King, and God of all people. This surah asks Him to protect us from whispers from jinn and people.

Key Lessons

  • Turn to Allah when bad thoughts or doubts come.
  • Allah controls and protects all people.
  • Faith gives the heart strength against whispers.
