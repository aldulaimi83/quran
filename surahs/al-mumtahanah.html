Surah 60 — الممتحنة

Surah Al-Mumtahanah — The Woman to be Examined

Read Surah Al-Mumtahanah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 60 of the Holy Quran — The Woman to be Examined — 13 verses.

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَتَّخِذُوا عَدُوِّي وَعَدُوَّكُمْ أَوْلِيَاءَ

Simple Meaning

This surah addresses the situation of Muslim women who emigrated from Makkah and their examination to verify their faith. It also discusses relationships with non-Muslims and cites Ibrahim as an example in not befriending those who openly oppose Allah.

Key Lessons

  • O believers, do not take My enemies and your enemies as allies (60:1).
  • Perhaps Allah will put affection between you and those whom you have considered enemies (60:7).
  • Allah does not forbid you from being kind and just to those who have not fought you over religion (60:8).
