Surah 114 — الناس

Surah An-Nas — Mankind

Read Surah An-Nas online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 114 of the Holy Quran — Mankind — 6 verses.

قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ النَّاسِ ۝ مَلِكِ النَّاسِ ۝ إِلَٰهِ النَّاسِ

Simple Meaning

The final surah and the second of the two protective surahs. It seeks refuge in Allah — the Lord of mankind, the King of mankind, the God of mankind — from the whispering of the retreating shaytan who whispers in the hearts of people.

Key Lessons

  • Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind — the King of mankind — the God of mankind (114:1-3).
  • From the evil of the retreating whisperer (114:4).
  • Who whispers in the hearts of mankind — from among jinn and men (114:5-6).
