Surah 35 — فاطر

Surah Fatir — The Originator

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ فَاطِرِ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with praise of Allah as the Originator of the heavens and the earth. It calls people to recognise that Allah alone provides and protects, and warns that Shaytan is an enemy who only invites people to the Fire.

Key Lessons

  • All praise is for Allah, Creator of the heavens and the earth (35:1).
  • O people, you are in need of Allah — and Allah is the Self-Sufficient, the Praiseworthy (35:15).
  • Indeed those who recite the Book of Allah, establish prayer, and spend from what We provided — they hope for a transaction that will never perish (35:29).
