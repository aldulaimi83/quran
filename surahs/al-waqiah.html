Surah 56 — الواقعة

Surah Al-Waqi'ah — The Inevitable Event

Read Surah Al-Waqi'ah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 56 of the Holy Quran — The Inevitable Event — 96 verses.

إِذَا وَقَعَتِ الْوَاقِعَةُ

Simple Meaning

This surah divides humanity on the Day of Judgment into three groups: the forerunners who are closest to Allah, the companions of the right, and the companions of the left. It describes the pleasures of Paradise and the torment of the Fire in vivid detail.

Key Lessons

  • When the inevitable event occurs — there is no denying its occurrence (56:1-2).
  • And you will be of three kinds (56:7).
  • Recite Al-Waqi'ah every night and poverty will never befall you (attributed tradition).
