Surah 25 — الفرقان

Surah Al-Furqan — The Criterion

Read Surah Al-Furqan online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 25 of the Holy Quran — The Criterion — 77 verses.

تَبَارَكَ الَّذِي نَزَّلَ الْفُرْقَانَ عَلَىٰ عَبْدِهِ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens by praising the Quran as the criterion between right and wrong. It describes the servants of the Most Merciful — those who walk humbly, speak peacefully, and spend neither wastefully nor miserly.

Key Lessons

  • Blessed is He who sent down the Criterion upon His servant (25:1).
  • The servants of the Most Merciful are those who walk on earth with humility (25:63).
  • Do not make your hand chained to your neck nor stretch it completely — lest you sit in reproach and destitution (17:29).
