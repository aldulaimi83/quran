بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ
إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ
اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ

Simple Meaning

This surah teaches that all praise belongs to Allah, the Lord of all worlds. It reminds us that Allah is Most Merciful, Master of the Day of Judgment, and the One we worship and ask for help.

Key Lessons

  • Begin with Allah's name and remember His mercy.
  • Ask Allah for guidance every day.
  • Worship Allah alone and rely on Him for help.
