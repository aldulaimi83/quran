Surah 108 — الكوثر

Surah Al-Kawthar — The Abundance

Read Surah Al-Kawthar online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 108 of the Holy Quran — The Abundance — 3 verses.

إِنَّا أَعْطَيْنَاكَ الْكَوْثَرَ ۝ فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَانْحَرْ ۝ إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ الْأَبْتَرُ

Simple Meaning

The shortest surah in the Quran. Allah tells the Prophet He has given him Al-Kawthar — a river in Paradise, great abundance, and prophethood. In response, the Prophet should pray and sacrifice. His enemies will be cut off.

Key Lessons

  • Indeed We have given you Al-Kawthar (108:1).
  • So pray to your Lord and sacrifice (108:2).
  • Indeed it is your enemy who is cut off (108:3).
