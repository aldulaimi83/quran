Surah 51 — الذاريات

Surah Adh-Dhariyat — The Winnowing Winds

Read Surah Adh-Dhariyat online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 51 of the Holy Quran — The Winnowing Winds — 60 verses.

وَالذَّارِيَاتِ ذَرْوًا

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with oaths by the winds, clouds, ships, and angels. It then tells the stories of Ibrahim's guests (the angels), Lut's people, Fir'awn, the 'Ad, and Thamud — all destroyed for rejecting their prophets.

Key Lessons

  • And I did not create the jinn and mankind except to worship Me (51:56).
  • And in the earth are signs for those who have certainty of faith (51:20).
  • By the winds that scatter — by the clouds heavy with rain — by the ships sailing with ease (51:1-3).
