Surah 26 — الشعراء

Surah Ash-Shu'ara — The Poets

وَإِنَّهُ لَتَنزِيلُ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ

Simple Meaning

One of the longer Meccan surahs, it retells the stories of Musa and Pharaoh, Ibrahim, Nuh, Hud, Salih, Lut, and Shu'ayb. Each story follows the same pattern — a prophet calls his people, they reject him, and punishment follows.

Key Lessons

  • Truly this is a revelation from the Lord of the worlds (26:192).
  • Your Lord is the Almighty, the Most Merciful (26:9).
  • The poets — the deviant follow them — they say what they do not do (26:224-226).
