Surah 37 — الصافات

Surah As-Saffat — Those Who Set the Ranks

Read Surah As-Saffat online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 37 of the Holy Quran — Those Who Set the Ranks — 182 verses.

وَالصَّافَّاتِ صَفًّا

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with an oath by those arranged in ranks — the angels. It retells the stories of Nuh, Ibrahim's near-sacrifice of his son, Ilyas, Lut, and Yunus. It affirms the oneness of Allah and the reward of the righteous.

Key Lessons

  • By those arranged in ranks (37:1).
  • Peace be upon Ibrahim — thus do We reward those who do good (37:109-110).
  • The Day when children shall be of no benefit, nor wealth — except to the one who comes to Allah with a sound heart (26:88-89).
