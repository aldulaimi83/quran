Surah 93 — الضحى

Surah Ad-Duha — The Morning Brightness

Read Surah Ad-Duha online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 93 of the Holy Quran — The Morning Brightness — 11 verses.

وَالضُّحَىٰ ۝ وَاللَّيْلِ إِذَا سَجَىٰ ۝ مَا وَدَّعَكَ رَبُّكَ وَمَا قَلَىٰ

Simple Meaning

Revealed when revelation paused for a time and the Prophet was distressed, this surah reassures him with great love — Your Lord has not abandoned you, nor hated you. It reminds him of how Allah found him an orphan and sheltered him.

Key Lessons

  • By the morning brightness — by the night when it settles (93:1-2).
  • Your Lord has not abandoned you, nor hated you (93:3).
  • Did He not find you an orphan and shelter you? (93:6).
