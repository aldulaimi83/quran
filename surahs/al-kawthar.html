Surah Al-Kawthar - Translation, Tafsir, Audio & Word-by-Word Learning

Read Surah Al-Kawthar online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word by word meaning about gratitude and Allah blessings. This page is designed to help readers listen Quran online, understand Quran with translation, and study Quran with tafsir in a simple format.

إِنَّا أَعْطَيْنَاكَ الْكَوْثَرَ
فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَانْحَرْ
إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ الْأَبْتَرُ

Simple Meaning

Allah gave the Prophet abundant good. The response to blessings is prayer, worship, sacrifice, and gratitude to Allah.

Key Lessons

  • Allah's blessings are greater than people's insults.
  • Show gratitude through worship.
  • Prayer connects the heart to Allah.
