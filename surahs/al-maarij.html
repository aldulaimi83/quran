Surah 70 — المعارج

Surah Al-Ma'arij — The Ascending Stairways

Read Surah Al-Ma'arij online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 70 of the Holy Quran — The Ascending Stairways — 44 verses.

سَأَلَ سَائِلٌ بِعَذَابٍ وَاقِعٍ

Simple Meaning

This surah describes a questioner who asked about a punishment to come. It explains that the Day of Judgment is as if fifty thousand years long — difficult for the disbelievers but easy for the believers. It describes the qualities of those who will inherit the Gardens.

Key Lessons

  • A questioner asked about a punishment to descend upon the disbelievers — no one can avert it (70:1-2).
  • Indeed man was created anxious — when evil touches him, he panics, and when good touches him, he withholds it — except those who pray (70:19-22).
  • And those in whose wealth there is a fixed right for the one who asks and the deprived (70:24-25).
