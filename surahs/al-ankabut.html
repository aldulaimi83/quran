Surah 29 — العنكبوت

Surah Al-Ankabut — The Spider

Read Surah Al-Ankabut online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 29 of the Holy Quran — The Spider — 69 verses.

أَحَسِبَ النَّاسُ أَن يُتْرَكُوا أَن يَقُولُوا آمَنَّا وَهُمْ لَا يُفْتَنُونَ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with the reality that faith is tested. It then discusses the stories of Ibrahim, Lut, Nuh, Hud, Salih, and Shu'ayb and compares the polytheist's false gods to a spider's web — fragile and without support.

Key Lessons

  • Do people think they will be left to say 'We believe' without being tested? (29:2).
  • The likeness of those who take protectors other than Allah is like the spider who builds a home — the spider's home is the frailest of homes (29:41).
  • Recite what has been revealed to you of the Book and establish prayer — prayer prevents indecency and wrongdoing (29:45).
