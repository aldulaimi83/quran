Surah 9 — التوبة

Surah At-Tawbah — The Repentance

فَإِن تَابُوا وَأَقَامُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتَوُا الزَّكَاةَ فَإِخْوَانُكُمْ فِي الدِّينِ

Simple Meaning

The only surah without Bismillah at the start. It addresses breaking of treaties with polytheists, the hypocrites, and the importance of Tawbah (repentance). It contains the famous Verse of the Sword and the story of the three companions left behind.

Key Lessons

  • If they repent, establish prayer and pay zakat, they are your brothers in faith (9:11).
  • Allah has purchased the lives of the believers in exchange for Paradise (9:111).
  • Allah turned to the three who stayed behind — indeed He is the Ever-Relenting (9:118).
