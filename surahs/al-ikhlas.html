Surah 112

Surah Al-Ikhlas - Translation and Tafsir

Surah Al-Ikhlas is a short and powerful chapter that explains pure belief in Allah's oneness.

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ
اللَّهُ الصَّمَدُ
لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ
وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ

Simple Meaning

Allah is One, complete, and independent. He has no children, no parents, and nothing is equal or comparable to Him.

Key Lessons

  • Allah alone deserves worship.
  • Allah is perfect and does not need anyone.
  • True faith starts with knowing Allah correctly.
