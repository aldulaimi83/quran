Surah 101 — القارعة

Surah Al-Qari'ah — The Calamity

Read Surah Al-Qari'ah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 101 of the Holy Quran — The Calamity — 11 verses.

الْقَارِعَةُ ۝ مَا الْقَارِعَةُ ۝ وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا الْقَارِعَةُ

Simple Meaning

This short surah describes the Day of Judgment — the Striking Calamity. People will be like scattered moths, mountains like carded wool. Whoever's scales are heavy with good deeds enters a pleasant life; whoever's are light, their home is an abyss.

Key Lessons

  • The Striking Calamity — what is the Striking Calamity? And what will make you know what the Striking Calamity is? (101:1-3).
  • As for one whose scales are heavy — he will have a pleasant life (101:6-7).
  • As for one whose scales are light — his home will be an abyss (101:8-9).
