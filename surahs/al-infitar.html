Surah 82 — الإنفطار

Surah Al-Infitar — The Cleaving

Read Surah Al-Infitar online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 82 of the Holy Quran — The Cleaving — 19 verses.

إِذَا السَّمَاءُ انفَطَرَتْ

Simple Meaning

This surah describes the Day when the sky splits, the stars scatter, the seas overflow, and the graves are overturned. It asks man what has deceived him about his Most Generous Lord and reminds him that noble guardian angels record all his deeds.

Key Lessons

  • When the sky is split open (82:1).
  • O man, what has deceived you concerning your Most Generous Lord? (82:6).
  • But truly over you are appointed guardians — noble, recording — they know all that you do (82:10-12).
