Surah 50 — ق

Surah Qaf — Qaf

Read Surah Qaf online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 50 of the Holy Quran — Qaf — 45 verses.

ق وَالْقُرْآنِ الْمَجِيدِ

Simple Meaning

This short powerful surah begins with the letter Qaf and an oath by the Glorious Quran. It speaks of the creation of man and the record kept by two guardian angels, the agony of death, and the trumpet blast of the Day of Judgment.

Key Lessons

  • Qaf — by the Glorious Quran (50:1).
  • We have created man and We know what his soul whispers to him — We are closer to him than his jugular vein (50:16).
  • There is no word he utters except that there is an observer beside him, ready (50:18).
