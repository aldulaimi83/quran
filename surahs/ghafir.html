Surah 40 — غافر

Surah Ghafir — The Forgiver

حم ۝ تَنزِيلُ الْكِتَابِ مِنَ اللَّهِ الْعَزِيزِ الْعَلِيمِ ۝ غَافِرِ الذَّنبِ

Simple Meaning

Also known as Surah Mu'min, this surah opens by describing Allah as the Forgiver of sin and the Accepter of repentance. It tells of the believing man from Pharaoh's family who secretly believed in Musa and spoke up to defend him.

Key Lessons

  • Allah is the Forgiver of sin, the Accepter of repentance, severe in punishment, and the Owner of abundance — there is no god but Him (40:3).
  • Call upon Me — I will respond to you (40:60).
  • And the believing man from Pharaoh's family said: O my people, follow me — I will guide you to the path of right conduct (40:38).
