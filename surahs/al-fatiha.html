Surah Al-Fatiha — The Opening

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ ۝ الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ ۝ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ ۝ مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ ۝ إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ ۝ اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ

Simple Meaning

The Opening is the most recited surah in the Quran, read in every prayer. It praises Allah as Lord of all worlds, the Most Merciful, and Master of Judgment Day, and asks Him for guidance on the straight path.

Key Lessons

  • Praise belongs to Allah alone — He is the Lord of everything.
  • Ask Allah for guidance in every prayer, every single day.
  • The straight path is the path of those Allah has blessed.
