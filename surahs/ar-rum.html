Surah 30 — الروم

Surah Ar-Rum — The Romans

Read Surah Ar-Rum online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 30 of the Holy Quran — The Romans — 60 verses.

الم ۝ غُلِبَتِ الرُّومُ ۝ فِي أَدْنَى الْأَرْضِ

Simple Meaning

This surah begins with a prophecy about the Roman Empire's defeat and then victory — which came true. It speaks of the signs of Allah in creation: the alternation of night and day, sleep, lightning, rain, and the variety of languages.

Key Lessons

  • And among His signs is that He created you from dust, then you became human beings spreading across the earth (30:20).
  • And among His signs is that He created for you spouses from among yourselves so that you may find peace with them (30:21).
  • Corruption has appeared in the land and sea because of what people's own hands have done (30:41).
