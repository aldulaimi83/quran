Surah Ta-Ha — Ta-Ha

طه ۝ مَا أَنزَلْنَا عَلَيْكَ الْقُرْآنَ لِتَشْقَىٰ

Simple Meaning

This surah addresses the Prophet directly, telling him the Quran was not revealed to cause him distress. It tells the story of Musa in great detail — his call at the burning bush, his confrontation with Pharaoh, and the golden calf.

Key Lessons

  • We have not sent down the Quran to cause you distress (20:2).
  • I am Allah — there is no god but Me, so worship Me and establish prayer (20:14).
  • My Lord, expand my chest, ease my task, and untie the knot in my tongue (20:25-28).
