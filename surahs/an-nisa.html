Surah 4 — النساء

Surah An-Nisa — The Women

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ اتَّقُوا رَبَّكُمُ الَّذِي خَلَقَكُمْ مِنْ نَفْسٍ وَاحِدَةٍ

Simple Meaning

This surah addresses the rights of women, orphans, inheritance, marriage, and justice. It calls believers to uphold trust, maintain justice even against themselves, and obey Allah and His Messenger.

Key Lessons

  • O people, be mindful of your Lord who created you from a single soul (4:1).
  • Do not consume the wealth of orphans wrongfully (4:2).
  • Allah commands you to return trusts to their rightful owners and to judge with justice (4:58).
