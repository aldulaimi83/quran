Surah 78 — النبأ

Surah An-Naba — The Great News

Read Surah An-Naba online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 78 of the Holy Quran — The Great News — 40 verses.

عَمَّ يَتَسَاءَلُونَ ۝ عَنِ النَّبَإِ الْعَظِيمِ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with the question: about what are they asking each other? The great news — the Day of Resurrection. It lists the signs of Allah in creation and then describes the Judgment Day and the contrast between Hell and Paradise.

Key Lessons

  • About what are they asking each other? About the great news (78:1-2).
  • Indeed the Day of Decision has been appointed (78:17).
  • Truly for the righteous there will be a place of success — gardens and vineyards (78:31-32).
