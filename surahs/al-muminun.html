Surah 23 — المؤمنون

Surah Al-Mu'minun — The Believers

قَدْ أَفْلَحَ الْمُؤْمِنُونَ ۝ الَّذِينَ هُمْ فِي صَلَاتِهِمْ خَاشِعُونَ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with a beautiful description of the successful believers — humble in prayer, avoiding vain speech, paying zakat, and guarding their chastity. It then tells the stories of Nuh, Musa, Isa, and ends with a powerful supplication.

Key Lessons

  • Successful indeed are the believers who are humble in their prayers (23:1-2).
  • My Lord, forgive and have mercy — You are the best of those who show mercy (23:118).
  • Do you think We created you without purpose and that you would not return to Us? (23:115).
