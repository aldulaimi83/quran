وَإِذْ قَالَ لُقْمَانُ لِابْنِهِ وَهُوَ يَعِظُهُ يَا بُنَيَّ لَا تُشْرِكْ بِاللَّهِ

Simple Meaning

This surah contains the beautiful wisdom of Luqman to his son — advice on avoiding shirk, being grateful, praying, commanding good and forbidding evil, and not walking arrogantly. It is full of timeless parenting guidance.

Key Lessons

  • O my son, do not associate anything with Allah — indeed shirk is a grave injustice (31:13).
  • O my son, establish prayer, command what is right, forbid what is wrong, and be patient over what happens to you (31:17).
  • Do not turn your face away from people arrogantly, and do not walk on earth with pride (31:18).
