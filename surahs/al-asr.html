وَالْعَصْرِ
إِنَّ الْإِنسَانَ لَفِي خُسْرٍ
إِلَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالْحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالصَّبْرِ

Simple Meaning

People are in loss unless they believe, do good deeds, advise each other to truth, and advise each other to patience.

Key Lessons

  • Time is valuable and should not be wasted.
  • Faith must be joined with good action.
  • Muslims help each other stay truthful and patient.
