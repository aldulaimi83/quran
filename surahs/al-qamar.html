Surah 54 — القمر

Surah Al-Qamar — The Moon

Read Surah Al-Qamar online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 54 of the Holy Quran — The Moon — 55 verses.

اقْتَرَبَتِ السَّاعَةُ وَانشَقَّ الْقَمَرُ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with the splitting of the moon — a miracle granted to the Prophet. It then rapidly retells the stories of Nuh, 'Ad, Thamud, Lut, and Pharaoh, each time repeating: We have made the Quran easy for remembrance — is there anyone who will remember?

Key Lessons

  • The Hour has drawn near and the moon was split (54:1).
  • And We have made the Quran easy for remembrance — is there anyone who will remember? (54:17).
  • Indeed the righteous will be in gardens and rivers, in a seat of honour with an All-Powerful King (54:54-55).
