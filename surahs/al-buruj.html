Surah 85 — البروج

Surah Al-Buruj — The Mansions of the Stars

Read Surah Al-Buruj online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 85 of the Holy Quran — The Mansions of the Stars — 22 verses.

وَالسَّمَاءِ ذَاتِ الْبُرُوجِ

Simple Meaning

This surah opens with an oath by the sky with its constellations. It tells of the People of the Pit — those who burned the believers for their faith. It affirms that the Quran is in a Preserved Tablet and that Allah is All-Encompassing in His punishment.

Key Lessons

  • By the sky with its constellations — by the promised Day (85:1-2).
  • Those who persecute believing men and women without repentance — they will have the punishment of Hell (85:10).
  • Indeed those who believe and do righteous deeds — for them are Gardens beneath which rivers flow (85:11).
