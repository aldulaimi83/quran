Surah 67

Surah Al-Mulk Benefits and Meaning

Surah Al-Mulk teaches that dominion belongs to Allah alone. It invites people to reflect on life, death, the heavens, and the consequences of disbelief and gratitude.

Arabic Text

تَبَارَكَ الَّذِي بِيَدِهِ الْمُلْكُ ... سُورَةٌ تذكر بقدرة الله وملكه التام

English Translation

The surah opens by declaring that all dominion is in Allah's hand. It explains that life and death are tests, invites reflection on creation, and warns people not to ignore the truth.

Word-by-Word Meaning

Al-Mulk means dominion or complete ownership. Biyadihi means in His hand. The surah repeatedly directs the reader to look, think, and reflect.

Tafsir (Simple Explanation)

Surah Al-Mulk is a chapter of perspective. It tells humans not to act like owners when they are only tested servants. Allah owns the world, controls life and death, and knows what hearts hide.

Benefits of Surah Al-Mulk

  • It strengthens awareness of Allah's control over creation.
  • It encourages regular reflection on the purpose of life.
  • It is one of the most commonly studied surahs for daily reflection.

Explanation for Kids

For children, Surah Al-Mulk can be explained as a chapter that teaches that Allah owns everything, made the sky beautifully, and wants people to live good lives before returning to Him.
