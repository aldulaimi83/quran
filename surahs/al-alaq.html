Surah 96 — العلق

Surah Al-Alaq — The Clot

Read Surah Al-Alaq online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 96 of the Holy Quran — The Clot — 19 verses.

اقْرَأْ بِاسْمِ رَبِّكَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ

Simple Meaning

The first five verses of this surah were the very first revelation to the Prophet. Read in the name of your Lord who created — who created man from a clot. It then warns against those who forbid the servant from prayer.

Key Lessons

  • Read in the name of your Lord who created — created man from a clinging clot (96:1-2).
  • Read — and your Lord is the Most Generous — who taught by the pen — taught man what he did not know (96:3-5).
  • No — if he does not stop, We will drag him by the forelock (96:15).
