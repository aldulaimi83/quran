Surah 59 — الحشر

Surah Al-Hashr — The Exile

Read Surah Al-Hashr online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 59 of the Holy Quran — The Exile — 24 verses.

سَبَّحَ لِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ

Simple Meaning

This surah describes the expulsion of Banu Nadir from Madinah. It ends with one of the most beautiful passages in the Quran — the names and attributes of Allah, culminating in: He is Allah, the Creator, the Originator, the Fashioner.

Key Lessons

  • He is Allah — there is no god but Him. He knows the unseen and the witnessed. He is the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful (59:22).
  • He is Allah, the Creator, the Originator, the Fashioner — to Him belong the most beautiful names (59:24).
  • If We had sent this Quran down upon a mountain, you would have seen it humbled and split apart from fear of Allah (59:21).
