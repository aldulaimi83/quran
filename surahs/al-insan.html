Surah 76 — الإنسان

Surah Al-Insan — The Human

Read Surah Al-Insan online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 76 of the Holy Quran — The Human — 31 verses.

هَلْ أَتَىٰ عَلَى الْإِنسَانِ حِينٌ مِّنَ الدَّهْرِ لَمْ يَكُن شَيْئًا مَّذْكُورًا

Simple Meaning

Also called Ad-Dahr (Time), this surah speaks of the creation of man, the path of guidance shown to him, and then contrasts the fate of the grateful believer and the ungrateful disbeliever. It describes Paradise in beautiful detail.

Key Lessons

  • Has there not come upon man a period of time when he was nothing to be mentioned? (76:1).
  • They feed food to the poor, the orphan, and the captive — saying: We only feed you for the sake of Allah — we seek no reward or thanks from you (76:8-9).
  • This is truly a reminder — so whoever wills, let them take a path to their Lord (76:29).
