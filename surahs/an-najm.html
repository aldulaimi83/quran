Surah 53 — النجم

Surah An-Najm — The Star

Read Surah An-Najm online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 53 of the Holy Quran — The Star — 62 verses.

وَالنَّجْمِ إِذَا هَوَىٰ ۝ مَا ضَلَّ صَاحِبُكُمْ وَمَا غَوَىٰ

Simple Meaning

Opening with an oath by the star, this surah affirms that the Prophet does not speak from desire — the Quran is revelation. It describes the Prophet's ascent and his vision, rejects the intercession of idols, and addresses the Hereafter.

Key Lessons

  • By the star when it descends — your companion has not strayed nor gone astray (53:1-2).
  • He does not speak from desire — it is only a revelation that is revealed (53:3-4).
  • That to your Lord is the final destination (53:42).
