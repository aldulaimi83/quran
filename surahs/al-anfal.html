Surah 8 — الأنفال

Surah Al-Anfal — The Spoils of War

إِنَّمَا الْمُؤْمِنُونَ الَّذِينَ إِذَا ذُكِرَ اللَّهُ وَجِلَتْ قُلُوبُهُمْ

Simple Meaning

Revealed after the Battle of Badr, this surah discusses the spoils of war, the qualities of true believers, and the importance of obedience to Allah and His Messenger in times of trial.

Key Lessons

  • True believers are those whose hearts tremble when Allah is mentioned (8:2).
  • Obey Allah and His Messenger and do not dispute with one another, lest you falter (8:46).
  • If you are patient and mindful of Allah, their plots will not harm you at all (8:66).
