Surah 109 — الكافرون

Surah Al-Kafirun — The Disbelievers

Read Surah Al-Kafirun online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 109 of the Holy Quran — The Disbelievers — 6 verses.

قُلْ يَا أَيُّهَا الْكَافِرُونَ ۝ لَا أَعْبُدُ مَا تَعْبُدُونَ

Simple Meaning

This surah is a clear declaration of the incompatibility of Islam and polytheism. It draws a firm line — there is no mixing, no compromise, no shared worship. It ends with the principle: for you is your religion and for me is mine.

Key Lessons

  • Say: O disbelievers — I do not worship what you worship (109:1-2).
  • Nor are you worshippers of what I worship (109:3).
  • For you is your religion and for me is my religion (109:6).
