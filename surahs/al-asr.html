Surah 103

Surah Al-Asr Meaning and Simple Explanation

Surah Al-Asr is a very short surah with a very big message: time matters, faith matters, and patience matters. This page gives simple tafsir and meaning in English.

Arabic Text

وَالْعَصْرِ
إِنَّ الْإِنسَانَ لَفِي خُسْرٍ
إِلَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالْحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالصَّبْرِ

English Translation

Allah swears by time and teaches that people are in loss unless they have faith, do righteous deeds, encourage truth, and encourage patience.

Word-by-Word Meaning

Al-asr means time. Khusr means loss. Amanu means they believed. Tawasaw means they advised one another.

Tafsir (Simple Explanation)

This surah gives a short formula for success: faith, action, truth, and patience. It warns that a life without these four things is a wasted life.

Benefits of Surah Al-Asr

  • It is easy to memorize and full of practical guidance.
  • It reminds believers to value time and use it well.
  • It teaches that real success is spiritual, not just worldly.

Explanation for Kids

Surah Al-Asr teaches children not to waste time. It says a good life needs belief in Allah, good actions, honesty, and patience.
