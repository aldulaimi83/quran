Surah 19 — مريم

Surah Maryam — Mary

وَاذْكُرْ فِي الْكِتَابِ مَرْيَمَ إِذِ انتَبَذَتْ مِنْ أَهْلِهَا

Simple Meaning

This surah tells of the miraculous birth of Isa (Jesus) to Maryam, the story of Zakariya and Yahya, and Ibrahim's conversation with his idol-worshipping father. It emphasises that Isa is a prophet and servant of Allah, not divine.

Key Lessons

  • She said: How can I have a son when no man has touched me? He said: Your Lord says it is easy for Me (19:20).
  • Isa said: Indeed I am the servant of Allah — He gave me the Book and made me a prophet (19:30).
  • Peace be upon him the day he was born, the day he dies, and the day he is raised alive (19:15).
