Surah 88 — الغاشية

Surah Al-Ghashiyah — The Overwhelming

Read Surah Al-Ghashiyah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 88 of the Holy Quran — The Overwhelming — 26 verses.

هَلْ أَتَاكَ حَدِيثُ الْغَاشِيَةِ

Simple Meaning

This surah begins: Has the news of the Overwhelming Event reached you? It then contrasts the fate of the humiliated disbelievers forced to eat bitter thorns against the believers reclining in a high garden. It ends with a reminder to ponder the signs of creation.

Key Lessons

  • Has the news of the Overwhelming Event reached you? (88:1).
  • Indeed your Lord is watchful (89:14).
  • Then do you not look at the camel — how it was created? And at the sky — how it was raised? (88:17-18).
