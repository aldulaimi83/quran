Surah 48 — الفتح

Surah Al-Fath — The Victory

Read Surah Al-Fath online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 48 of the Holy Quran — The Victory — 29 verses.

إِنَّا فَتَحْنَا لَكَ فَتْحًا مُّبِينًا

Simple Meaning

Revealed after the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah, this surah declares it a clear victory even though it appeared to be a setback. It describes the pledge under the tree and the qualities of the Companions of the Prophet.

Key Lessons

  • Indeed We have granted you a clear victory (48:1).
  • Muhammad is the Messenger of Allah, and those with him are forceful against the disbelievers, merciful among themselves (48:29).
  • It is He who sent down tranquillity into the hearts of the believers (48:4).
