Surah 106 — قريش

Surah Al-Quraysh — Quraysh

Read Surah Al-Quraysh online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 106 of the Holy Quran — Quraysh — 4 verses.

لِإِيلَافِ قُرَيْشٍ ۝ إِيلَافِهِمْ رِحْلَةَ الشِّتَاءِ وَالصَّيْفِ

Simple Meaning

This very short surah reminds the Quraysh — guardians of the Kaaba — to worship Allah for the two blessings of their safe trade journeys in winter and summer, and for their safety and protection from hunger and fear.

Key Lessons

  • For the accustomed security of the Quraysh — their accustomed security in journeys of winter and summer (106:1-2).
  • Let them worship the Lord of this House — who fed them against hunger and made them safe from fear (106:3-4).
  • Gratitude to Allah is the foundation of every blessing.
