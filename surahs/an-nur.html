Surah 24 — النور

Surah An-Nur — The Light

Read Surah An-Nur online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 24 of the Holy Quran — The Light — 64 verses.

اللَّهُ نُورُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ

Simple Meaning

This surah contains rules about modesty, false accusations of adultery, entering homes with permission, and the famous Light Verse describing Allah's light. It calls believers to lower their gaze and guard their chastity.

Key Lessons

  • Allah is the Light of the heavens and the earth (24:35).
  • Tell believing men to lower their gaze and guard their private parts (24:30).
  • O you who believe, do not enter homes other than your own without first asking permission (24:27).
