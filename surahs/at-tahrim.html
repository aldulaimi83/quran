Surah 66 — التحريم

Surah At-Tahrim — The Prohibition

Read Surah At-Tahrim online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 66 of the Holy Quran — The Prohibition — 12 verses.

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ لِمَ تُحَرِّمُ مَا أَحَلَّ اللَّهُ لَكَ

Simple Meaning

This surah addresses an incident involving the Prophet and his household. It calls believers to repent sincerely and presents two examples of disbelieving women (the wives of Nuh and Lut) and two examples of believing women (the wife of Pharaoh, Asiya, and Maryam).

Key Lessons

  • O believers, repent to Allah with sincere repentance — perhaps your Lord will remove your sins (66:8).
  • My Lord, build for me near You a house in Paradise and save me from Pharaoh and his deeds (66:11).
  • Allah presents an example of those who believed — the wife of Pharaoh (66:11).
