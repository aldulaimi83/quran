Surah 61 — الصف

Surah As-Saf — The Ranks

Read Surah As-Saf online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 61 of the Holy Quran — The Ranks — 14 verses.

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لِمَ تَقُولُونَ مَا لَا تَفْعَلُونَ

Simple Meaning

This surah rebukes those who say what they do not do. It recalls how Isa (Jesus) gave glad tidings of a prophet named Ahmad who would come after him. It calls believers to support the cause of Allah like soldiers in a firm rank.

Key Lessons

  • O you who believe, why do you say what you do not do? (61:2).
  • And Isa son of Maryam said: O Children of Israel, I am the messenger of Allah to you, confirming what came before me and giving good news of a messenger to come after me, whose name is Ahmad (61:6).
  • Stand in ranks as if you are one solid structure (61:4).
