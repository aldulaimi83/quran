Surah 83 — المطففين

Surah Al-Mutaffifin — The Defrauders

Read Surah Al-Mutaffifin online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 83 of the Holy Quran — The Defrauders — 36 verses.

وَيْلٌ لِّلْمُطَفِّفِينَ

Simple Meaning

This surah begins with a severe warning against those who give less than due when measuring or weighing for others. It goes on to describe the record of the wicked and the record of the righteous, and the reward of the believers in the Garden.

Key Lessons

  • Woe to those who defraud in weight and measure (83:1).
  • Indeed the record of the wicked is in Sijjin (83:7).
  • Indeed the righteous will be in bliss — reclining on couches, looking around (83:22-23).
