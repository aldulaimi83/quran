Surah 77 — المرسلات

Surah Al-Mursalat — The Emissaries

Read Surah Al-Mursalat online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 77 of the Holy Quran — The Emissaries — 50 verses.

وَالْمُرْسَلَاتِ عُرْفًا

Simple Meaning

This powerful surah opens with oaths by the winds and angels sent in succession. It then warns of a coming event — the Day of Judgment — and repeats throughout: Woe that Day to the deniers! It calls people to reflect on the signs.

Key Lessons

  • By those sent forth in succession — by the raging storms (77:1-2).
  • Did We not create you from a despicable fluid? (77:20).
  • Woe that Day to the deniers! (77:15, repeated 10 times).
