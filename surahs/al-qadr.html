Surah 97 — القدر

Surah Al-Qadr — The Night of Decree

Read Surah Al-Qadr online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 97 of the Holy Quran — The Night of Decree — 5 verses.

إِنَّا أَنزَلْنَاهُ فِي لَيْلَةِ الْقَدْرِ ۝ وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا لَيْلَةُ الْقَدْرِ ۝ لَيْلَةُ الْقَدْرِ خَيْرٌ مِّنْ أَلْفِ شَهْرٍ

Simple Meaning

This short but mighty surah reveals that the Quran was sent down on the Night of Decree — a night better than a thousand months. The angels and Jibril descend with every decree, and it is a night of peace until the break of dawn.

Key Lessons

  • Indeed We sent the Quran down in the Night of Decree (97:1).
  • The Night of Decree is better than a thousand months (97:3).
  • Peace it is — until the rise of dawn (97:5).
