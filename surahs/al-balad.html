Surah 90 — البلد

Surah Al-Balad — The City

Read Surah Al-Balad online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 90 of the Holy Quran — The City — 20 verses.

لَا أُقْسِمُ بِهَٰذَا الْبَلَدِ

Simple Meaning

This surah swears by Makkah and speaks of man being created in hardship and struggle. It presents two paths — the steep path of freeing slaves, feeding orphans and the poor — or the path of denial. It asks: have We not given him two eyes and a tongue?

Key Lessons

  • I swear by this city — Makkah — and you are lawful in this city (90:1-2).
  • We have shown him the two paths (90:10).
  • But he did not attempt the steep path — and what will make you know what the steep path is? — freeing a slave, or feeding on a day of hunger an orphan of close relation or a poor person in misery (90:11-16).
