Surah 55 — الرحمن

Surah Ar-Rahman — The Beneficent

الرَّحْمَٰنُ ۝ عَلَّمَ الْقُرْآنَ ۝ خَلَقَ الْإِنسَانَ ۝ عَلَّمَهُ الْبَيَانَ

Simple Meaning

One of the most loved surahs, Ar-Rahman lists the blessings of Allah and asks 31 times: Which of your Lord's favours will you deny? It speaks of the creation of man from clay and jinn from smokeless fire, and describes the two levels of Paradise.

Key Lessons

  • The Most Merciful — taught the Quran — created man — taught him speech (55:1-4).
  • So which of the favours of your Lord will you deny? (55:13, repeated 31 times).
  • Is the reward of goodness anything but goodness? (55:60).
