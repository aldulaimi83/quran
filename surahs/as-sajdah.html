Surah 32 — السجدة

Surah As-Sajdah — The Prostration

Read Surah As-Sajdah online with English translation, tafsir, audio recitation, and word-by-word Quran learning. Surah 32 of the Holy Quran — The Prostration — 30 verses.

تَتَجَافَىٰ جُنُوبُهُمْ عَنِ الْمَضَاجِعِ يَدْعُونَ رَبَّهُمْ

Simple Meaning

This short surah speaks of the revelation of the Quran, the creation of man from clay, and the stages of the afterlife. It describes the believers who rise at night to pray to their Lord in hope and fear.

Key Lessons

  • Their sides leave their beds to call upon their Lord in fear and hope (32:16).
  • No soul knows what joy is hidden for them as a reward for what they used to do (32:17).
  • Is the one who believes equal to the one who transgresses? They are not equal (32:18).
